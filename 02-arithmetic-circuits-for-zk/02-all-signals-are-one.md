# All signals are one

## Problem

Create an arithmetic circuit that takes signals $x_1, x_2, â€¦, x_n$ and is satsified if all signals are $1$.

## Solution

### If domain is $x_i \in \{0,1\}$

$1 \equiv x_1 \cdot x_2 \cdot ... \cdot x_n$

### If domain is not defined

$x_1 \equiv 1$

$x_2 \equiv 1$

$...$

$x_n \equiv 1$
